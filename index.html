About

Established in Sanford in the spring of 2023, Carolina Lakes Exterior Services is a veteran owned small business serving Sanford, Fayetteville, and surrounding communities. We specialize in residential and commercial exterior cleaning services. Our expert technicians are dedicated to providing the highest value service to our customers. We provide a wide range of exterior cleaning services to keep your property maintained, maximize longevity of components such as roofs and windows, and, most importantly, keep your house clean so it looks and feels like home!


We offer several packages, and are happy to work within your budget to determine the best service option for you. Call us today for a free estimate!
